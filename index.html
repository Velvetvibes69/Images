Facts about Seoul City

Seoul is the capital city of South Korea. It is a modern, busy city known for its tall buildings, fast technology, and rich history. Famous places include Gyeongbokgung Palace, N Seoul Tower, and the Han River. People enjoy K-pop, Korean food like kimchi and bibimbap, and shopping in places like Myeongdong. It is a city that mixes old traditions with modern life.
